ATTATHI DEVO BHAVA

India is an unequivocally welcoming country, that bestows respect and love for its visitors. A nation which follows only one belief blindly “Atithi Devo Bhava” which means “Guest is equivalent to God”. Land of diverse cultures, deep-rooted beliefs, uncountable religions, that is connected to specific historical tales as well; there is so much to explore. An avid traveller can merely find its abode here; India Tourism offers an excellent rich heritage, delectable cuisines from different parts of the country, a plethora of alluring spots for sightseeing, and many more when exploring this beautiful country.
